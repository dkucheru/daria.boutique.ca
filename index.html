Return Policy

At Daria.boutique.ca, we prioritize quality and strive to ensure that each item meets our high standards before it reaches you. Every item is carefully inspected and video-recorded prior to shipping to confirm its quality and condition.

Return Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • No-Reason Returns: You may request a return without explanation within 5 days of delivery. The item must:
    • Be unworn.
    • Have all original tags attached.
    • Be in perfect condition (no stains, damages, or odors).

    Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.

  • Our Mistake: If we sent the wrong size, wrong color, or a damaged item:
    • Notify us within 3 days of receiving the product.
    • Provide a description and photos for verification.
    • Return shipping costs will be covered by us, and we will issue a refund or replacement upon verification.

Important Notes

Refunds will only be processed once the returned item passes quality checks.

Items that do not meet the above conditions will be returned to the customer, and no refund will be issued.

Customers are responsible for ensuring correct size and color selection. If unsure, please consult with our team before purchasing.

Variations in color due to screen differences are not eligible for returns.

Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Reach out to us at [your contact information] within the specified timeframe with relevant details and photos.
  2. Return Authorization: If eligible, we will provide instructions for the return process.
  3. Inspection & Refund: Once the item is received and inspected, we will process your refund.
